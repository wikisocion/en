Here at the type domains you can get a taste of the world of each of the socionic types. We ask that people contribute only to the domain of their own type and comment on other type domains on the discussion pages only.

We also have recently added quadra domains where representatives of all four quadra types can contribute, and a related "type talk" project discusses what people of the same type talk about when they get together.

General guidelines

The goal of these pages is to introduce readers to the world of each type (or quadra) — how the type experiences reality and interacts with the rest of the world. Do with that what you wish; socionic analysis is not necessary. Disputes about page content — if any — will be resolved by representatives of the type who have been widely recognized as belonging to the type. The administrator will intervene only if page development is clearly going against the stated purpose of this section.
