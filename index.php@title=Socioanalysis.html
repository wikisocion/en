Socioanalysis is a concept established by Gulenko, which stipulates that society can be analysed with socionics.

It includes 29 sub-concepts, with slightly definitions. (definitions must be translated)

  1. Socionics
  2. Sociotype
  3. Sociomodel
  4. Socion
  5. Intertype relations
  6. Rings
  7. Quadras
  8. Evolution
  9. Involution
  10. Statics
  11. Dynamics
  12. Linear-assertive temperament
  13. Flexible-maneuvering temperament
  14. Receptive-adaptive temperament
  15. Balanced-stable temperament
  16. Club of pragmatists
  17. Club of researchers
  18. Club of humanitarians
  19. Club of socials
  20. Logic
  21. Ethics
  22. Sensing
  23. Intuition
  24. Extraversion
  25. Introversion
  26. Rationality
  27. Irrationality
  28. Central society
  29. Peripheral society

source : http://www.socioniko.net/ru/articles/notion.html
