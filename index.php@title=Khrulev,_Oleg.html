Khrulev, Oleg

From Wikisocion
Jump to: navigation, search

Oleg Anatolievich Khrulev is a socionist from Moscow, Russia, and the owner of numerous online socionics resources, including, as of May 2007, Socioniko.net, a multi-language resource on socionics. Khrulev also created a Benchmark list project. Oleg is planning a number of new socionics projects in English for 2007, including a forum and a website with descriptions. Self-types as ILI.

Biography

  • Born in 1977.
  • Graduated from the Faculty of Computational Mathematics and Cybernetics.
  • Now is studying in graduate economic university. Works as a programmer.

Has been engaged with socionics since 1998. Currently developing a socionics website together with psychologist Elena Zamanskaya. Collecting a database which presents the views of various socionists on types of famous people with help of Dmitry Lytov.

Other interests include psychology, yoga, football, chess.

Website Projects

www.socionic.ru – news from projects of psychology and socionics.
www.sociolove.ru – socionics dating website with forum, chat, and in-built feature to search people by TIM among other factors.
forum.sociolove.ru – socionics forum for meeting new people.
www.socioforum.ru – forum "Psychology, socionics, sociology, philosophy", place to interact for people from various socionics clubs and schools.
www.humo.ru – humor on subjects of psychology and socionics, typing stories and anecdoes, guides for dual pairs.
www.famo.ru – socionics types of famous people, search feature for type databases of more than 40 socionists.
www.geshtalt.ru – socionics and gestalt, articles on socionics, psychology, psycho-linguistics, and gestalt for beginners.
www.tests-tests.ru – socionics and psychological tests.
www.socioclub.ru – general information about club "Quadra".
www.sociotype.ru – descriptions of socionics types created by Elena Zamanskaya and Oleg Khrulev.
